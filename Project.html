Westfield Shopping Center

Panoramic Commercial

San Francisco, California

Project Overview

A series of 12 panoramic glass elevators installed throughout this premium shopping destination, offering both functionality and aesthetic appeal. The project involved complex integration with the existing structure while maintaining the center's operations.

Client

Westfield Corporation

Completion Date

March 2023

Project Duration

8 months

Project Value

$4.2 million

Technical Specifications

Elevator Type Panoramic Glass
Capacity 1,600 kg / 21 persons
Speed 1.75 m/s
Travel Height 45 meters
Number of Stops 5 floors
Drive System Machine Room-Less Gearless Traction

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Installing glass elevators in an operational shopping center without disrupting business activities.

Solution: Implemented a phased installation approach with most work conducted during off-hours. Custom hoistway enclosures were pre-fabricated off-site to minimize on-site construction time.

Challenge: Integrating modern elevators with the existing building management system.

Solution: Developed a custom interface module that allowed seamless communication between our elevator control systems and the building's existing infrastructure.
