About Seyon

Discover our journey of innovation, meet our team, and learn about the values that drive our success in the vertical transportation industry.

Our Story

Founded in 2000 by a team of visionary engineers with decades of combined experience in the elevator industry, Seyon began with a simple mission: to revolutionize vertical transportation through innovation, quality, and exceptional service.

What started as a small operation with just 15 employees has grown into a global enterprise with over 500 team members across 12 countries. Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to our founding principles while continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of our clients and the industry.

Today, Seyon is recognized as an industry leader, having completed more than 1,500 projects worldwide, from luxury hotels and commercial skyscrapers to healthcare facilities and industrial complexes. Our success is built on our unwavering commitment to safety, reliability, and cutting-edge technology.

Richard Anderson

Founder & CEO

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our core principles, we strive to set new standards in the elevator industry.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, safe, and reliable vertical transportation solutions that exceed client expectations through engineering excellence, superior craftsmanship, and exceptional service.

We are committed to enhancing the built environment by creating elevator systems that seamlessly integrate with architectural designs while prioritizing safety, efficiency, and user experience.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in vertical transportation solutions, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainability, while setting new standards for safety and performance in the industry.

We envision a future where Seyon elevators are the preferred choice worldwide, known for their reliability, energy efficiency, and contribution to smart building technologies.

Excellence

Our Journey Through the Years

Key milestones that have shaped our company's growth and success.

2000

Seyon founded by Richard Anderson with a team of 15 engineers in New York City.

2005

Completed our first major project - a 40-story commercial tower with 12 high-speed elevators.

2008

Expanded operations to Europe with the opening of our London office.

2012

Launched our revolutionary energy-efficient elevator system, reducing power consumption by 40%.

2015

Celebrated the installation of our 1000th elevator and expanded to Asia with offices in Singapore and Shanghai.

2018

Introduced our smart elevator technology with IoT integration and predictive maintenance capabilities.

2020

Celebrated 20 years in business and launched our touchless elevator control system in response to global health concerns.

2023

Completed our most ambitious project to date - the elevator system for the 120-story Global Finance Tower.

2025

Celebrating 25 years of excellence with the launch of our next-generation sustainable elevator platform.
